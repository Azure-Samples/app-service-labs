# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Developers, cloud engineers, architects, students, and workshop authors who want
practical experience building, deploying, securing, and operating apps on Azure
App Service.

## Product Purpose

Azure App Service Labs provides hands-on labs and connected learning paths that
turn Azure App Service concepts into working, reproducible experience. Success
means learners can quickly find the right lab, complete it with confidence, and
understand how the capability applies to a production app.

## Brand Personality

Practical, welcoming, and credible. The site should feel distinctly Microsoft
and Azure while remaining approachable for learners at different skill levels.

## Anti-references

- A visual clone of another Azure lab site.
- Generic science imagery such as test tubes used as the main identity.
- Dense enterprise marketing pages that obscure the hands-on content.
- Novelty styling that weakens trust, readability, or accessibility.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the outcome learners will achieve.
- Make Microsoft and Azure provenance clear without overwhelming the content.
- Use visual personality to aid orientation, not decorate every surface.
- Keep navigation and procedures scannable, predictable, and inclusive.
- Show production practices through working examples and honest constraints.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Maintain sufficient contrast, semantic structure, descriptive
alt text and links, keyboard access, input-neutral instructions, responsive
layouts, and reduced-motion alternatives for meaningful animation.
